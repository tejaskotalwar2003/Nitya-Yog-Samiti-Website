Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Who can join yoga classes?+

Anyone including students, professionals and senior citizens can join.

Do beginners need prior yoga experience?+

No experience is required. Beginners are guided step by step.

What is the timing of yoga sessions?+

Morning sessions usually start at 5:00 AM followed by pranayama and meditation.

Is there any fee for yoga classes?+

Most sessions are community service based. Some workshops may have minimal fees.

What should I bring to yoga class?+

A yoga mat, comfortable clothes and a water bottle.

Can senior citizens participate?+

Yes, yoga is suitable for people of all ages.

How can I register for yoga classes?+

You can register through our website or visit the nearest branch.

Are yoga classes conducted every day?+

Yes, most branches conduct yoga sessions daily.

Can students join yoga classes?+

Yes, students are encouraged to practice yoga for better focus and health.

How can I contact Nitya Yog Samiti?+

You can contact us through phone or the contact page.

Health & Practice Questions

What are the benefits of yoga?+

Yoga improves flexibility, strength, mental peace and overall health.

Can yoga help reduce stress?+

Yes, yoga and meditation help reduce stress and anxiety.

Do you provide yoga training for beginners?+

Yes, beginner friendly sessions are conducted.

Are yoga instructors experienced?+

Yes, experienced instructors guide the sessions.

Do you organize yoga events?+

Yes, yoga camps and International Yoga Day events are organized.

Can yoga help in managing health conditions?+

Yoga can support management of stress, anxiety and lifestyle diseases.

Is yoga safe for people with medical issues?+

People with serious medical conditions should consult a doctor first.

How long does it take to see results?+

Many people notice improvements within a few weeks of regular practice.

What is the difference between yoga and meditation?+

Yoga includes physical postures while meditation focuses on calming the mind.

Can yoga improve mental health?+

Yes, yoga helps improve emotional balance and concentration.
