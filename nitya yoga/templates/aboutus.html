About Nitya Yog Samiti

Who We Are

Nitya Yog Samiti is a yoga organization dedicated to promoting a healthy, disciplined and spiritually balanced lifestyle. Through regular yoga practice, pranayama and meditation we help individuals achieve physical health, mental peace and spiritual growth.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a healthy and spiritually aware society through yoga and meditation.

Mission

To spread yoga awareness and encourage people of all ages to practice yoga daily.

Objectives

Promote yoga awareness and motivate people to adopt a healthy lifestyle.

Our Guru

Shri Sitaram Ji Sontakke

Shri Sitaram Ji Sontakke is the guiding force behind Nitya Yog Samiti. He has dedicated his life to spreading yoga awareness and helping people achieve physical health, mental peace and spiritual development.
